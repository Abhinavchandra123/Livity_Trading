About Us

LIVITY EQUIPMENT TRADING

Livity Equipment Trading is one of the most credible innovative traders of Construction materials, Mechanical and Electrical Equipment. With a strong basement and skilled operation, our company can supply the materials anywhere around UAE as per customer requirement within a confirmed schedule and a cost-effective solution. The Company prides itself in its business practices. Our foremost business objective is to provide enhanced products and excellent service to our valuable customers which add significance to their business.We specialize in Industrial service and trading having representation from reputed manufacturers to supply Industrial Insulation & Accessories, Valves & Gaskets, Pipes Fittings, Jacketing & Accessories.We are dedicated to high-quality products, stock availability and an extremely resourceful after sales service to our clients with a wider range of products to satisfy the requirements of their complete project

The company has the proficiency and the aptitude to supply all Electromechanical needs for major projects. We bring you a broad base of scientific and technical knowledge to draw on in the Electromechanical and building material applications.
